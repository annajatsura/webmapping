This is a map that shows a few of the different amenities that are available to people who live in or are visiting the Washington DC area. Narrow down what you want to see by selecting different features.

There are bicycle hubs where you can rent a bike, get repairs, and more. There’s also a handful of theatres that have all kinds of shows from classic plays to more contemporary productions

If you’re looking for a fun night out, Washington DC has got you covered with a variety of bars. There’s plenty to choose from whether you’re looking for a quiet spot to sit and enjoy time with friends or a lively dance floor.

If you’re a music lover, there are a few awesome sores to check out! From vinyl records to CDs to instruments, there’s a store for every type of music enthusiast.
